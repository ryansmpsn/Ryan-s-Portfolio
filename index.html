About Me

Hello, my name is Ryan Simpson. I am a Software Engineer specializing in Front-End Development, based in Jacksonville, FL.

I was born and raised in Jacksonville, Florida. I began my journey when I moved to Illinois to attend Aurora Univerity originally for physical therapy. It was there that I sparked my interest for development when I took a programming class as an elective. From then I knew this is what I wanted to do. Having no real programming experience prior I was at a slight disadvantage compared to the general population but I used this to fuel my hunger for learning and now I love everything about making computers work.
